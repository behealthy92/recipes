    Garlic Sautéed Red Rice Bowl¶

    A warm, customizable bowl with a garlicky red rice base, roasted veggies, and a protein of your choice.

    Quick facts¶

    Prep time
    15 minutes
    Cook time
    25 minutes
    Yield
    1 serving

    Recipe flow¶

    1. Cook the red rice base.
    2. Roast the vegetables while the rice cooks.
    3. Choose a protein and cook it.
    4. Whisk the sauce and assemble the bowl.

    A) Garlic Sautéed Red Rice (Base)¶

    Ingredients¶

    Ingredient Quantity Notes
    Red rice (dry) 70 g
    Water 120–130 ml
    Garlic 2 cloves Minced
    Button mushrooms 80 g Sliced
    Egg 1
    Oil 1 tbsp
    Seasoning To taste Salt + black pepper
    Mixed herbs 1/2 tsp Optional

    Steps¶

    1. Cook rice. Rinse red rice, cook with water, then fluff and rest for 5 minutes.
    2. Sauté aromatics. In a medium pan, heat oil, sauté garlic for 10 seconds, then mushrooms for 2–3 minutes.
    3. Scramble egg. Push the veggies aside and scramble the egg in the same pan.
    4. Stir-fry. Add cooked rice, seasoning, and mixed herbs (if using). Stir-fry 2–3 minutes.

    B) Veg Toppings (Same Every Time)¶

    Ingredients & prep¶

    Ingredient Quantity Prep
    Broccoli 150 g Season with 1 clove minced garlic, 1 tsp oil, salt & pepper.
    Mushrooms 80–120 g Season with 1 tsp oil, salt & pepper.
    Corn 70 g Steam or microwave for 2–3 minutes if frozen.

    Roast instructions¶

    1. Preheat oven to 200°C.
    2. Roast broccoli and mushrooms for 12–15 minutes, flipping halfway through.

    C) Protein (Pick 1)¶

    Ingredients

    Ingredient Quantity Notes
    Chicken breast (raw) 130 g
    Garlic 1 clove Minced
    Oil 1 tsp
    Seasoning To taste Salt + pepper (optional paprika)

    Steps

    1. Rub the chicken with oil, garlic, salt, and pepper/paprika.
    2. Roast at 200°C for 15–18 minutes (same oven as the vegetables).
    3. Rest 5 minutes, then slice.

    Ingredients

    Ingredient Quantity Notes
    Tiger prawns (raw) 90 g Peeled
    Garlic 1 clove Minced
    Oil (or butter) 1 tsp
    Seasoning To taste Salt + pepper (optional lemon)

    Steps

    1. Pat prawns dry.
    2. In a medium pan, heat oil/butter, sauté garlic for 10 seconds.
    3. Cook prawns 1.5–2 minutes per side until pink.

    D) Sauce¶

    Ingredients¶

    Ingredient Quantity Notes
    Lemon juice 1 tbsp (15 ml)
    Light soy sauce 1 tbsp (15 ml)
    Olive oil 1 tsp (5 ml)
    Honey 1/2 tsp
    Garlic 1 clove Minced
    Black pepper To taste

    Instructions¶

    1. Whisk all ingredients together until the honey dissolves.
    2. Drizzle over the assembled bowl just before serving.

    Assemble the bowl¶

    1. Spoon the red rice into a bowl.
    2. Add roasted vegetables, corn, and your chosen protein.
    3. Finish with sauce and extra herbs if desired.
