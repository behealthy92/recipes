# Garlic Sautéed Red Rice Bowl

A warm, customizable bowl with a garlicky red rice base, roasted vegetables, and a protein of your choice, finished with a bright sauce.

---

## Equipment

- Small saucepan with lid  
- Medium skillet / sauté pan  
- Sheet pan  
- Mixing bowl + whisk  
- Cutting board + knife

## Instructions

### 1) Start the rice (hands-off simmer)

1. **Rinse** red rice in a fine sieve until the water runs mostly clear (30–60 sec).
2. **Add to saucepan:** rinsed rice + **120–130 ml water** + **pinch of salt** (optional).
3. **Bring to a gentle boil** over medium-high heat (watch closely).
4. **Cover + reduce to low** the moment it starts bubbling.
5. **Simmer 25–30 min** (avoid opening the lid).
6. **Check doneness:** grains should be tender with a pleasant chew.  
   - If still firm: add **1–2 tbsp hot water**, cover, simmer **3–5 min** more.
7. **Rest 5 min off heat**, then fluff with a fork.

!!! tip "Rice texture control"
    If your red rice often turns out dry, start with **130 ml** water. If it turns mushy, use **120 ml**.

---

### 2) Preheat oven + prep the veg

1. **Preheat oven:** 200°C (400°F).
2. **Line a sheet pan** (optional for easier cleanup).
3. **Broccoli bowl:** add broccoli + **1 tsp oil** + minced garlic + **salt + pepper**. Toss well.
4. **Mushroom bowl:** add mushrooms + **1 tsp oil** + **salt + pepper**. Toss well.
5. **Spread on pan** in a single layer (don’t overcrowd; use 2 pans if needed).

---

### 3) Roast the veg (while rice cooks)

1. **Roast 12–15 min**, flipping once at the halfway mark.
2. **Stop when:** broccoli has browned edges and mushrooms look glossy + browned.

!!! tip "Better browning"
    Keep broccoli pieces similar size and leave space between veg (steam = less browning).

---

### 4) Protein (pick your tab)

=== "Roasted Chicken Breast"
    !!! warning "Food safety"
        Chicken is cooked when the thickest part hits **74°C / 165°F**.

    1. **Pat dry** chicken breast (better browning).
    2. **Rub seasoning:** oil + garlic + salt + pepper over all sides.
    3. **Roast at 200°C** for **15–18 min** (timing depends on thickness).
    4. **Rest 5 min** (juicier), then slice.

=== "Garlic Tiger Prawns"
    1. **Pat dry** prawns very well (prevents steaming).
    2. **Season:** salt + pepper.
    3. **Heat pan** over medium-high, add oil/butter.
    4. **Garlic 10 seconds** (fragrant, not browned).
    5. **Cook prawns:** ~**1.5–2 min per side** until pink and opaque.
    6. **Optional:** finish with a squeeze of lemon.

!!! tip "Avoid rubbery prawns"
    Pull them the moment they turn opaque—carryover heat finishes the job.

=== "Grilled Salmon"
    !!! tip "Doneness guide"
        Salmon is usually great at **52–57°C** (medium to medium-well).  
        For a fully cooked texture, go closer to **60–63°C**.

    1. **Pat dry** salmon fillet (crispier exterior).
    2. **Season:** salt + pepper (and optional garlic).
    3. **Preheat pan:** medium-high, add oil/butter.
    4. **Sear first side:** place salmon **presentation-side down** (or skin-side down if skin-on). Cook **3–4 min** until nicely browned.
    5. **Flip:** cook **2–4 min** more (time depends on thickness) until flaky at the edges.
    6. **Rest 2 min**, then break into chunks.
    7. **Optional:** finish with lemon squeeze.
    
---

### 5) Optional add-in (pick 0–1)

=== "Corn"
    1. **Microwave/steam 2–3 min** if frozen.
    2. **Drain well** and set aside.

=== "Edamame"
    1. **Microwave/steam 2–3 min** if frozen.
    2. **Drain well** and set aside.

=== "Cherry tomatoes"
    1. **Halve** and set aside.
    2. **Season (optional):** pinch of salt + pepper.

---

### 6) Sauce (pick 1)

=== "Lemon Soy"
    1. Add all ingredients to a bowl/jar.
    2. **Whisk 30–60 sec** until honey dissolves.
    3. **Taste + adjust:** more lemon for brightness, more soy for salt.

=== "Miso Brown Sugar"
    1. Add all ingredients to a bowl.
    2. **Whisk 30–60 sec** until smooth (no miso lumps).
    3. **Taste + adjust:** more vinegar for tang, more sugar for sweetness.

!!! tip "Make it smoother"
    If miso clumps, add **1 tsp warm water**, then whisk again.

---

### 7) Finish the base — garlic sautéed rice

1. **Heat skillet** over medium, add **1 tbsp olive oil**.
2. **Garlic 10 seconds** (just fragrant).
3. **Add mushrooms** and sauté **2–3 min** until softened and glossy.
4. **Push mushrooms aside** to create a clear spot in the pan.
5. **Crack in egg**, scramble **1–2 min** until just set.
6. **Add cooked rice** + measured salt + pepper.
7. **Stir-fry 2–3 min** until hot, lightly glossy, and evenly mixed.
8. **Taste + adjust** salt/pepper.

!!! tip "If your rice clumps"
    Add **1 tsp water** and stir-fry another **30 sec** to loosen.

---

## Assemble

1. **Base:** spoon garlic rice into a bowl.
2. **Veg:** add roasted broccoli + roasted mushrooms.
3. **Add-in:** corn / edamame / tomatoes (optional).
4. **Protein:** top with chicken or prawns.
5. **Sauce:** drizzle generously.
6. **Finish (optional):** extra lemon squeeze, cracked pepper, chopped herbs.
