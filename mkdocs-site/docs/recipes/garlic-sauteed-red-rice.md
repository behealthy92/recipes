# Green Monster Salad

This recipe consists of a red rice base, roasted vegetable toppings, and a choice of protein.

## A) Garlic Sautéed Red Rice (Base)

### Ingredients

| Ingredient | Quantity | Notes |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Red rice (dry) | 70 g | |
| Water | 120–130 ml | |
| Garlic | 2 cloves | Minced |
| Button mushrooms | 80 g | Sliced |
| Egg | 1 | |
| Oil | 1 tbsp | |
| Seasoning | To taste | Salt + black pepper |
| Mixed herbs | ½ tsp | Optional |

### Steps

1. **Cook Rice:** Rinse red rice, then cook with water. Fluff and rest for 5 minutes.
2. **Sauté:** In a medium pan, add oil, then garlic for 10 seconds, and then mushrooms for 2–3 minutes.
3. **Scramble Egg:** Push the sautéed ingredients aside and scramble the egg.
4. **Stir-fry:** Add the cooked rice, salt/pepper, and mixed herbs (if using). Stir-fry for 2–3 minutes.

## B) Veg Toppings (Same Every Time)

### Ingredients & Prep

| Ingredient | Quantity | Prep |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Broccoli | 150 g | Season with 1 clove minced garlic, 1 tsp oil, salt & pepper. |
| Mushrooms | 80–120 g | Season with 1 tsp oil, salt & pepper. |
| Corn | 70 g | Steam or microwave for 2–3 minutes if frozen. |

### Roast Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 200°C.
2. Roast the seasoned broccoli and mushrooms for 12–15 minutes (flip once halfway through).

## Protein Choices (Pick 1)

### Option 1: Roasted Chicken Breast (~90 g cooked)

**Ingredients**

| Ingredient | Quantity | Notes |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Chicken breast (raw) | 130 g | |
| Garlic | 1 clove | Minced |
| Oil | 1 tsp | |
| Seasoning | To taste | Salt + pepper (optional paprika) |

**Steps**

1. Rub the raw chicken with oil, garlic, salt, and pepper/paprika.
2. Roast at 200°C for 15–18 minutes (in the same oven as the vegetables).
3. Rest for 5 minutes, then slice.

### Option 2: Garlic Tiger Prawns (~60 g cooked)

**Ingredients**

| Ingredient | Quantity | Notes |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Tiger prawns (raw) | 90 g | Peeled |
| Garlic | 1 clove | Minced |
| Oil (or butter) | 1 tsp | |
| Seasoning | To taste | Salt + pepper (optional lemon) |

**Steps**

1. Pat the prawns dry.
2. In a medium-high pan, add oil/butter, then garlic for 10 seconds.
3. Cook the prawns for 1.5–2 minutes per side until pink. Remove from heat.

## Sauce

### Ingredients

| Ingredient | Quantity | Notes |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Lemon Juice | 1 tbsp (15 ml) | |
| Light Soy Sauce | 1 tbsp (15 ml) | |
| Olive Oil | 1 tsp (5 ml) | |
| Honey | 1/2 tsp | |
| Garlic | 1 clove | Minced |
| Black Pepper | To taste | |

### Instructions

1. Whisk all ingredients together in a small bowl until the honey is dissolved.
2. Drizzle over the assembled rice bowl before serving.
